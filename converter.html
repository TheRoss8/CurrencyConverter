Currency Converter

How does it work?


First of all, you have to input the data by simply clicking (or tapping) onto the field labeled "Amount to convert", then you just have to select the base currency (the one you want to convert from) and finally the target value (the one you want to convert to). The result will magically appear into the second box, and it updates in real time as you edit the amount (almost).
